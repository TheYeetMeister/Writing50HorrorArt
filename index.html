About Me

Welcome To My Website About Horror!

Photography gallery with certain filters and editing to create dark and formless images that are meant to take relatively mundane and average photos and twist them into obscurity and twist the colors and lines to make normality into depravity. These images depict ideas of Family, security, the great beyond, or ideas of the human mind and play with them in a way as to reveal, and exaggerate imperfections or flaws in otherwise safe or normal environments. The overall purpose of this art gallery is to shift the perspective of how people may see things or places, revealing to people the fear of the unknown, or shedding fear on what they thought they knew. I drew heavy inspiration from Laura Zalenga's "G O P L E", and Brian Somerville's "The visionary" as I framed my photos much like Laura Zalenga would photograph things with somewhat mundane or simple scenarios, besides a few photos, I attempted to try and make the photos relatively normal or mundane, while somerville's artistic skills took those relatively simple concepts and ideas and twisted them in creepy ways, as he added symbols on his art, I changed the line weight and shading of my photos to give off a sureal or off puttting atmosphere much like Somerville.
