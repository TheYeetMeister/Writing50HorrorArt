Praying...

This artwork depicts a person, perhaps a spirit, creature, or intruder slouching down on the floor facing what appears to be a towel, with the towel casting a shadow and a light or figure can be seen on the towel. The muted colors and rough outlines let the audience fill in the blanks of what the colors and room may actually look like from a realistic perspective, leaving room for the audience's imagination to run wild while also leaving many unknown factors for the audience to attempt to answer. The environment of the artwork appears to be taking place in a room in a home, most likely a bathroom as the towel rag and circular like object at the bottom, which appears to be a toilet or a small table, hints at the fact that the piece of art takes place in the bathroom. The overall image appears to give a eerie, or creepy aura, as what was once considered a safe area, as the bathroom is where people require much privacy, is invaded by a human-like creature that appears to be attempting to communicate to something, as the towel appears to act as a window to somewhere else with a figure present at the otherside of the towel. This artwork appears to play off the audience insecurity as what was once considered a safe haven appears to be invaded by two creatures, the creature slouching and the figure on the other side of the towel, and with not much known about the situation or just what is on the other side of the towel the audience is left to guess at what has entered the home.
