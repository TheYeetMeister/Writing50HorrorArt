Invader

This image illustrates a deep dark sea in the middle of a desolate land, with what appears to be creatures that are turtle-like existing at the bottom of the sea. This depicts an unnatural glowing ooze in the lake as well as shadows that appear to be grabbing, corporeal, and ethereal, this evokes a feeling of awe at what there is, but also a feeling of fear as it is unknown of what the forms and feelings of the creatures that exist in this ethereal pool of glowing ooze. The line work of this artwork also appears to depict the rocks or shadows to be alive, with shadows of other creatures like ducks existing in this swamp, the lighter line work of these rocks or ducks makes them appear ghostlike as if they exist in another plane of existence altogether that the audience can't quite comprehend. This artwork plays heavily onto the audience's fear of the unknown, as the swamp appears to be ethereal and ghost like, while the surroundings appear hellish, as if this artwork is a depiction of the afterlife, or more accurately hell, and is perhaps trying to warn the audience of what may be instore past their lives.
