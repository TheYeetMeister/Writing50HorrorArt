Praying...

This image depicts a teddy bear slouching over and staring at several photos on a window sill. The darkened lighting and lack of colors gives off a feel of history or something old, as if the drawing or photo exists in the past. The overall image feels homely and comfortable as the items and environment appear to be something out of an ordinary home, but the colors and position of the teddy bear and pictures gives off a feeling of distance, not a distance of space but of time, as if the audience is looking at the image through a TV or distance memory as the lines of the image are rough mimicking TV static or the numerous holes that exist in the minds of humans. This piece of art ties into the idea of the family by depicting how many family members look at the good old times, many people look back at their lives or families through a lens whether that be nostalgia or sentimentality, many look back on what they once was with imperfections in their memories in fear or regret at what they have become. The teddy bear is most likely a representation of these people, as Teddy Bears are what kids bring everywhere and are something children are very close with, with the Bear now alone and looking back what once was parallels with how people look back at their past and their families.
